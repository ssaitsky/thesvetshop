The origin story

26 years.
One volcano.
One hour.

In eighth grade, the boys at school started making fun of my name. Svetlana. So they called me Svet shirt. Svet pants. Svet mobile. Svet shop. Everything with a Svet in front of it.

So I said: oh, you just wait.

I said I was going to open the Svet Shop. A play on sweatshop. I was going to make the most beautiful things, and with all the money I'd make, I'd get rid of sweatshirts everywhere.

I talked about it for years. Decades, honestly. A lot of ideas came and went, and none of them were really it.

Then one night, sitting on a volcano in San Marcos La Laguna, Guatemala, a little elevated, a lot inspired, it became obvious: I already had the mantras. I already had the words. I'd been collecting them for years — in my art, in my work, in everything I do.

All I needed was a super soft cotton t-shirt as the canvas.

It only took me 26 years to build this in an hour. Life is funny.

— Svetlana

I started doodling my words and feelings as a kid and never stopped. Two decades of coaching later, my whole life still revolves around words — finding them, listening to them, sharing them with people who needed them and didn't know it. One of my core values is beauty. So a shirt that is comfortable, looks great, and carries words worth living by? That's the trifecta. The svet shop... created itself when it was ready. In one hour. On top of a volcano. You know how it goes…
