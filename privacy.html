Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

What we collect

When you make a purchase, we collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information. Payment details are processed securely by Stripe and never stored on our servers. When you sign up for email updates, we collect your email address only.

How we use it

We use your information to fulfill orders, send order confirmations, and occasionally send you updates about new mantras or offers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as needed to fulfill your order (Stripe for payments, Printful for fulfillment).

Email

If you subscribe to our email list, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send. We use Kit (formerly ConvertKit) to manage our email list.

Cookies

We use a single cookie to remember whether you've seen our welcome popup. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Third-party services

We use Stripe (payments), Printful (order fulfillment), and Kit (email). Each has their own privacy policy. We also use Google Analytics to understand site traffic.

Your rights

You can request to see, update, or delete your personal information at any time by emailing us at hello@svetlanasaitsky.com.
